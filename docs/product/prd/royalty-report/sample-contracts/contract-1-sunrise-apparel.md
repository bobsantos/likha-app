# LICENSING AGREEMENT

**Agreement Number:** BC-2024-0042

**This Licensing Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of **January 1, 2025** ("Effective Date") by and between:

**LICENSOR:**
BrandCo Holdings LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, with its principal place of business at 100 Brand Avenue, Suite 400, New York, NY 10001 ("Licensor")

**LICENSEE:**
Sunrise Apparel Co., a California corporation, with its principal place of business at 2250 Fashion District Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90021 ("Licensee")

---

## 1. GRANT OF LICENSE

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Licensor hereby grants to Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the BrandCo trademark, trade name, and associated brand identifiers (collectively, the "Licensed Property") solely in connection with the manufacture, marketing, distribution, and sale of licensed branded apparel products (the "Licensed Products") within the Territory defined herein.

This license does not include the right to sublicense, assign, or transfer any rights granted hereunder without the prior written consent of Licensor.

---

## 2. TERRITORY

The license granted under this Agreement is limited to the **United States of America**, including its territories and possessions ("Territory"). Licensee shall not sell, distribute, or authorize the sale of Licensed Products outside the Territory.

---

## 3. PRODUCT CATEGORIES

The license granted herein covers the following product category:

- **Apparel** (general) — including but not limited to tops, bottoms, outerwear, activewear, and accessories integral to apparel sets bearing the Licensed Property

Any expansion of product categories requires a written amendment to this Agreement signed by both parties.

---

## 4. TERM

This Agreement shall commence on **January 1, 2025** and shall expire on **December 31, 2027** ("Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 11 of this Agreement. Renewal of this Agreement, if any, shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the parties no later than ninety (90) days prior to the expiration of the then-current Term.

---

## 5. ROYALTIES

### 5.1 Royalty Rate

Licensee shall pay Licensor a royalty equal to **eight percent (8%)** of Net Sales of Licensed Products during each Reporting Period ("Royalty").

### 5.2 Royalty Base — Net Sales Defined

"Net Sales" means gross invoiced sales of Licensed Products less the following deductions, to the extent actually incurred and documented:

- Customer returns and credits actually credited to customers
- Trade allowances and promotional allowances agreed to in writing
- Freight charges separately stated on invoices

No other deductions shall be taken from gross sales in computing Net Sales. Royalties are not deductible from Net Sales.

### 5.3 Calculation

Royalty Due = Net Sales x 8%

---

## 6. MINIMUM GUARANTEE

### 6.1 Annual Minimum

Licensee guarantees that royalties paid to Licensor shall not be less than **Twenty Thousand Dollars ($20,000.00)** per contract year ("Annual Minimum Guarantee"). A "contract year" means each consecutive twelve (12)-month period beginning on January 1 and ending on December 31 during the Term.

### 6.2 Shortfall Payment

If, at the end of any contract year, the total royalties actually paid for that year are less than the Annual Minimum Guarantee, Licensee shall pay the shortfall amount (the difference between the Annual Minimum Guarantee and actual royalties paid) within thirty (30) days following the end of that contract year.

---

## 7. ADVANCE PAYMENT

### 7.1 Amount

Upon execution of this Agreement, Licensee shall pay Licensor a non-refundable advance against future royalties in the amount of **Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000.00)** ("Advance").

### 7.2 Recoupment

The Advance shall be recoupable against royalties earned under this Agreement during the first contract year only. Once royalties earned exceed the Advance amount, all subsequent royalties shall be payable as earned with no further offset.

---

## 8. REPORTING

### 8.1 Reporting Frequency

Licensee shall submit written royalty reports on a **quarterly** basis. Each report shall cover one calendar quarter as follows:

| Quarter | Period | Report Due |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Q1 | January 1 – March 31 | April 30 |
| Q2 | April 1 – June 30 | July 31 |
| Q3 | July 1 – September 30 | October 31 |
| Q4 | October 1 – December 31 | January 31 |

### 8.2 Report Contents

Each royalty report shall include:

- Reporting period start and end dates
- Gross sales of Licensed Products by product category
- Returns and allowances deducted
- Net Sales
- Applicable royalty rate
- Total royalty due
- Cumulative year-to-date royalties paid

### 8.3 Payment Terms

Royalty payments shall accompany each report and are due **no later than thirty (30) days after the end of the reporting period**. Payments not received by the due date shall accrue interest at the rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month on the outstanding balance.

---

## 9. AUDIT RIGHTS

Licensor shall have the right, upon reasonable advance written notice of not less than fifteen (15) business days, to audit Licensee's books and records relating to the sale of Licensed Products no more than once per contract year. If an audit reveals an underpayment of royalties greater than five percent (5%) of the amount due, Licensee shall bear the reasonable cost of such audit in addition to paying the deficiency plus interest.

---

## 10. QUALITY STANDARDS

Licensee shall submit samples of all Licensed Products to Licensor for written approval prior to commercial production. Licensee shall maintain quality standards consistent with approved samples throughout the Term and shall not materially alter the Licensed Products without Licensor's prior written approval.

---

## 11. TERMINATION

### 11.1 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within such notice period.

### 11.2 Effect of Termination

Upon expiration or termination, Licensee shall immediately cease manufacturing Licensed Products. Licensee shall have sixty (60) days to sell off existing inventory ("Sell-Off Period"), subject to full royalty payment on all sell-off sales. Following the Sell-Off Period, all unsold Licensed Products shall be destroyed.
