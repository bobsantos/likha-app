# LICENSING AGREEMENT

**Agreement Number:** BC-2024-0078

**This Licensing Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of **March 1, 2025** ("Effective Date") by and between:

**LICENSOR:**
BrandCo Holdings LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, with its principal place of business at 100 Brand Avenue, Suite 400, New York, NY 10001 ("Licensor")

**LICENSEE:**
Meridian Goods LLC, a Texas limited liability company, with its principal place of business at 850 Commerce Street, Suite 200, Dallas, TX 75202 ("Licensee")

---

## 1. GRANT OF LICENSE

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Licensor hereby grants to Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the BrandCo trademark, trade name, and associated brand identifiers (collectively, the "Licensed Property") solely in connection with the manufacture, marketing, distribution, and sale of licensed products across the product categories specified in Section 3 ("Licensed Products") within the Territory defined herein.

This license does not include the right to sublicense, assign, or transfer any rights granted hereunder without the prior written consent of Licensor.

---

## 2. TERRITORY

The license granted under this Agreement is limited to the **United States and Canada** ("Territory"). For purposes of royalty calculation, all Canadian sales shall be converted to United States Dollars (USD) using the average exchange rate published by the Bank of Canada for the applicable reporting period. Licensee shall not sell, distribute, or authorize the sale of Licensed Products outside the Territory without prior written consent from Licensor.

---

## 3. PRODUCT CATEGORIES AND ROYALTY RATES

The license granted herein covers the following product categories, each subject to its own royalty rate as set forth in **Exhibit A** attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference:

| Product Category | Royalty Rate |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Apparel | 10% of Net Sales |
| Accessories | 12% of Net Sales |
| Footwear | 8% of Net Sales |

"Apparel" includes tops, bottoms, outerwear, and activewear. "Accessories" includes headwear, bags, keychains, and small leather goods. "Footwear" includes all licensed footwear styles. Any product that spans more than one category shall be classified by mutual written agreement of the parties prior to commercial production.

Any expansion of the product categories listed above requires a written amendment to this Agreement signed by both parties.

---

## 4. TERM

This Agreement shall commence on **March 1, 2025** and shall expire on **February 28, 2028** ("Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 11 of this Agreement. Renewal of this Agreement, if any, shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the parties no later than ninety (90) days prior to the expiration of the then-current Term.

---

## 5. ROYALTIES

### 5.1 Royalty Rate

Licensee shall pay Licensor a royalty on Net Sales of Licensed Products during each Reporting Period, calculated separately by product category at the rates set forth in Section 3 and Exhibit A.

### 5.2 Royalty Base — Net Sales Defined

"Net Sales" means gross invoiced sales of Licensed Products less the following deductions, to the extent actually incurred and documented:

- Customer returns and credits actually credited to customers
- Trade allowances and promotional allowances agreed to in writing
- Freight charges separately stated on invoices

No other deductions shall be taken from gross sales in computing Net Sales. Royalties are not deductible from Net Sales.

### 5.3 Calculation by Category

Total Royalty Due = Sum of (Net Sales per category x Applicable rate per category)

Royalties shall be computed and reported separately for each product category. A blended average rate applied to total Net Sales across all categories shall not be an acceptable calculation method.

---

## 6. MINIMUM GUARANTEE

### 6.1 Annual Minimum

Licensee guarantees that royalties paid to Licensor shall not be less than **Fifty Thousand Dollars ($50,000.00)** per contract year ("Annual Minimum Guarantee"). A "contract year" means each consecutive twelve (12)-month period beginning on the first day of the month in which the Effective Date falls.

For the first contract year (March 1, 2025 – February 28, 2026), the full Annual Minimum Guarantee of $50,000.00 applies without proration.

### 6.2 Shortfall Payment

If, at the end of any contract year, the total royalties actually paid for that year are less than the Annual Minimum Guarantee, Licensee shall pay the shortfall amount within thirty (30) days following the end of that contract year. Shortfall amounts are non-recoupable and shall not be credited toward royalties in subsequent periods.

---

## 7. ADVANCE PAYMENT

### 7.1 Amount

Upon execution of this Agreement, Licensee shall pay Licensor a non-refundable advance against future royalties in the amount of **Twelve Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($12,500.00)** ("Advance").

### 7.2 Recoupment

The Advance shall be recoupable against royalties earned under this Agreement during the first contract year only. Once royalties earned exceed the Advance amount, all subsequent royalties shall be payable as earned with no further offset. The Advance shall not be applied against any minimum guarantee shortfall payment.

---

## 8. REPORTING

### 8.1 Reporting Frequency

Licensee shall submit written royalty reports on a **quarterly** basis. Each report shall cover one calendar quarter:

| Quarter | Period | Report Due |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Q1 | January 1 – March 31 | May 15 |
| Q2 | April 1 – June 30 | August 15 |
| Q3 | July 1 – September 30 | November 15 |
| Q4 | October 1 – December 31 | February 15 |

### 8.2 Report Contents

Each royalty report shall include:

- Reporting period start and end dates
- Gross sales of Licensed Products broken out by product category and SKU
- Returns and allowances deducted, itemized by category
- Net Sales by category
- Applicable royalty rate per category
- Royalty due per category
- Total royalty due for the period
- Cumulative year-to-date royalties paid
- For Canadian sales: the exchange rate applied and the original CAD amounts

### 8.3 Payment Terms

Royalty payments shall accompany each report and are due **no later than forty-five (45) days after the end of the reporting period**. Payments not received by the due date shall accrue interest at the rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month on the outstanding balance.

---

## 9. AUDIT RIGHTS

Licensor shall have the right, upon reasonable advance written notice of not less than fifteen (15) business days, to audit Licensee's books and records relating to the sale of Licensed Products no more than once per contract year. If an audit reveals an underpayment of royalties greater than five percent (5%) of the amount due, Licensee shall bear the reasonable cost of such audit in addition to paying the deficiency plus interest.

---

## 10. QUALITY STANDARDS

Licensee shall submit pre-production samples of all Licensed Products to Licensor for written approval prior to commercial production. Licensee shall maintain quality standards consistent with approved samples throughout the Term. Licensor shall have the right to inspect Licensed Products in the market and to require recall of non-conforming products at Licensee's expense.

---

## 11. TERMINATION

### 11.1 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within such notice period. Failure to pay royalties or submit required reports on time shall constitute a material breach.

### 11.2 Effect of Termination

Upon expiration or termination, Licensee shall immediately cease manufacturing Licensed Products. Licensee shall have sixty (60) days to sell off existing inventory ("Sell-Off Period"), subject to full royalty payment on all sell-off sales. Following the Sell-Off Period, all unsold Licensed Products shall be destroyed.
